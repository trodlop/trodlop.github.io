Las Palmas

A Minecraft city project by Tristan Rodriguez

Overview

Las Palmas is a solo city project by me, Tristan Rodriguez, built within Minecraft.

The project aims to be as realistic as possible, and such, the city follows construction and urban planning practices seen in refernce cities. The architecture of Las Palmas is influenced by cities such as Valencia, Monaco, Barcelona, New York and San Sebastian.

Las Palmas is a small, coastal city on a fictional island in the Mediterranean Sea. The vision for the city is walkablility and sustinability, for which special attention is given during planning.

The project was inspired by the city of Andea made by Cubed Community.

Las Palmas is currently on Minecraft 1.19.4 and uses a custom resource pack.

Planning

In order to assist with the construction of the city, I had to create an urban planning document, which you can download and see on the right.

The urban planning document gives guidance on current and future planning of Las Palmas, for example, outlining how the road network should be layed out and considerations for designing new buildings.

While it is not complete, the document has helped with the construction immensely, and has allowed steady progress of the project to be made.
