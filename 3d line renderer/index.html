This project will render any number of lines and vertices in 3D space. Choose a preset or create your own custom geometry

Note: the renderer does not perform back-face culling, and hence if a custom shape with many vertices is rendered,

screen may become very populated, and/or may affect performace. Please email me about any bugs.

This project is a work in progress and further features will be added in the future
